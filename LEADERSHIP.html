PRAYER FOR LEADERSHIP  
O’LORD, TODAY  
YOU BLESSED ME  
WITH KNOWLEDGE  
UNBEKNOWEST TO ME BEFORE  
I THANK YOU LORD  
LORD PLEASE SHARE  
WITH ME YOUR  
ENLIGHTENMENT  
AND UNDERSTANDING  
OF THE 4 FACES  
SPOKEN OF IN  
EZEKIEL 1:10/ EZEKIEL 10:14/ REVELATION 4:7  
EACH FACE IS A METAPHOR  
OF WHAT A LEADER/MANAGER  
NEEDS TO BE – ALL AT THE SAME TIME.  
THE LION: IMPLIES STRENGTH AND CONFIDENCE  
THE OX: IMPLIES WORK, SERVICE AND DEDICATION  
THE MAN: IMPLIES HUMANITY AND CARING  
THE EAGLE: KEEN VISION AND AWARE NESS OF BOTH IMMEDIATE AND FUTURE ENVIROMENT.  
O’LORD, PLEASE INSTILL IN ME THESE  
4 FACES OF EZEKIEL DAILY  
IN JESUS’ NAME, I PRAY  
AMEN  
BY: REGINALD TAYLOR
