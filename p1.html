prayer

Faithlessness

Dear God,

  • Cease ye from man;
  • in every situation,
  • I chose,
  • to trust man over you,
  • please remind me,
  • man's breath,
  • is in his nostril's.
  • I know lord,
  • who you are,
  • I can always,
  • trust in you God.
  • to trust in man,
  • only end in disappointment,
  • I know that in my,
  • my many examples,
  • of faithlessness,
  • wherein I am
  • to be accounted of,
  • Lord, I am grateful
  • that I can trust in you,
  • completely and not to worry
  • about you changing on me.
  • You are always faithful;
  • In Jesus' name
  • I pray,
  • Amen.
    • by: Reginald Taylor .
