Dear God; Heavenly Father:  
The Most High,  
And Lofty One.  
Aion – The Supreme Being…  
I ask that you,  
Remind me daily…  
To carry out,  
What you have called,  
Me to do…  
Please keep my heart,  
In great spirits…  
This work,  
I must do…  
Will never become,  
A burden…  
Only as a blessing.  
It is a honor, and privilege,  
To do your will,  
For you…  
I claim you,  
As my brother,  
As my sister,  
As my mother…  
As I attempt,  
To follow the will,  
Of God.  
Help me to truly,  
Allow you to lead me…  
On the path to righteousness,  
Please walk with me,  
As I attempt to follow.,  
Your will…  
The wonderful counselor:  
The prince of peace:  
Emanuel; Emanuel:  
His Spirit of Truth:  
The Divine Teacher:  
The Mighty Holy Spirit:  
I pray inJesus’name.  
AMEN! -  
By: Reginald Taylor
