Heavenly Father;  
The Most High,  
And Lofty One;  
One in the One  
I thank you,  
That the things,  
That comes from you,  
Are good and true…  
May you help me,  
To discern.. What is from you,  
And that which is not.  
I pray that my mind,  
Will always stay fixed,  
On you O’Lord.  
I will trust, In you completely.  
Father may I never,  
Pay attention to any distractions.  
That may come my way..  
May my gaze, Be fully fixed,  
On you, Lord.  
In Jesus’ name; I pray. The wonderful counselor:  
The Prince of Peace: Emanuel; Emanuel: His Spirit of Truth: The divine teacher:  
The great comforter: The Mighty Holy Spirit:  
I give thanks; To the Most High; And his Son; For acknowledgement, In answering my prayer.  
AMEN!  
By: Reginald Taylor
