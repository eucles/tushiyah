Father in Heaven:  
My most High,  
And Lofty One.  
One in the One:  
The Supreme Being:  
I give thanks unto you,  
Your patience is everlasting,  
Please, help me with mine.  
You never punish me,  
Because the choices we make,  
Becomes a dream of knowledge,  
Or a nightmare from hell.  
Here I pray for your,  
Mercy and grace.  
I believe in my disobedience.  
Punishment is just due.  
Through your forgiveness,  
And mercy, I am saved.  
None of this do I deserve.  
Give me the patience,  
You have for me,  
To share with others,  
Whom paths I cross.  
In the name of Jesus  
I pray:  
The Wonderful Counselor:  
The Prince of Peace:  
Emanuel; Emanuel:  
His Spirit of Truth:  
The Divine Teacher:  
The Great Comforter:  
The Mighty Holy Spirit:  
I give thanks;  
To My Most High;  
His Son;  
For acknowledgement, In responding to my prayer……AMEN  
BY:REGINALD TAYLOR
