Heavenly Father: The Most High;  
And Lofty One;  
I want to be pleasing,  
In your sight. Help me…  
To do the right thing,  
Help me to show other,  
As you have shown me,  
Your mercy… Help me to walk,  
A humble spirit, in your way.  
Lord, you asked the children.  
To come to you;  
Then you blessed them.  
I need to have the faith, Of a little child.  
To belong to the, Kingdom of God.  
Please, bless me with, Innocent faith;  
Of a child… Trusting in you.  
And your Love for me,  
In the name of Jesus’, the wonderful counselor,  
The Prince of Peace, Emanuel, Emanuel.  
His spirit of truth the divine teacher,  
The great comforter, The Mighty Holy Spirit.  
I give thanks, to the most High, to his Son,  
For acknowledgement; in answering my prayers. AMEN!  
By: Reginald Taylor
