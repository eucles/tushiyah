GODS PLAN  
DEAR GOD,  
THERE ARE  
MANY DEVICES  
IN A MANS HEART;  
BUT GOD ONLY YOU KNOW  
WHAT IS TRULY BEST FOR US.  
I COMMIT ALL MY PLANS  
DREAMS, AND ASPIRATIONS  
IN YOUR HANDS.  
IT IS, THE COUNSEL OF YOU  
O’LORD THAT SHALL STAND.  
THESE ARE ONLY PRODUCTS  
OF MY THOUGHTS.  
I TRULY WANT  
WHAT YOU WANT FOR ME,  
I AM SURE  
THAT IS WHAT WILL  
TRULY BE BEST FOR ME.  
IN JESUS’ NAME,  
I PRAY, AMEN.  
BY: REGINALD TAYLOR
