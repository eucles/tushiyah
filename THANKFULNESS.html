THANKFULNESS  
Heavenly Father;  
The Most High:  
And Lofty One;  
Pleroma-One in the One  
Aion-Supreme Deity:  
I thank you,  
For who you are…  
Always ready,  
To assure us…  
Of your goodness,  
And your mercy…  
Thank you, Father:  
That I am always,  
A recipient…  
Of your faithfulness,  
My life is nothing…  
Without you, O’Lord,  
Compassionate and forgiving:  
I ask that you keep me,  
On the right path…  
And when I fail,,  
Please forgive me my weakness…  
And lead me home.  
In Jesus’ name I pray  
The wonderful counselor  
The prince of peace  
Emanuel, Emanuel:  
His spirit of truth,  
The divine teacher,  
The great comforter,  
The Mighty Holy Spirit…  
I give thanks,  
To my most High;  
His son…  
For acknowledgement:  
In answering my prayers. AMEN!  
By:Reginald Taylor
