SALVATION  
I will greatly rejoice  
In the Lord.  
I am truly grateful,  
For this gift,  
Of salvation.  
My soul shall be,  
Joyful in my God.  
I thank you,  
For your Son,  
Jesus Christ.  
For he has clothed me,  
With the garments,  
Of salvation.  
Who has saved me,  
Cleanse me,  
And made me new,  
Before you.  
He hath covered me,  
With the robe of righteousness.  
I am free,  
From the weight,  
Of my old life.  
As a bridegroom,  
Decketh himself,  
With ornaments.  
I can live,  
Happily,  
In this new life .  
Amen!  
By: Reginald Taylor
