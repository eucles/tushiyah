Heavenly Father:  
My Most High,  
And Lofty One…  
Aion: Supreme Being…  
I give praise,  
To you with,  
Thankfulness;  
That you are God;  
Of All:..  
Your words are,  
Always kind,  
Always right,  
Always willing,  
To do what’s justifiable  
For me…  
Please forgive my sins,  
That are in my heart…  
I pray for your forgiveness.  
I understand the door,  
Is always open,  
At your house.  
There I can confess  
That you will be  
Faithful to hear me..  
For give me O’lord,  
For the sins - I’m coherent of;  
And the sins… that are incoherent…  
In the name of Jesus’  
I pray:- The wonderful counselor,  
The Prince of Peace:-Emanuel; Emanuel.  
His Spirit of Truth:-The Divine Teacher.  
The Mighty Holy Spirit:…  
I ask of your grace  
Most High, and your Son:  
For acknowledgement,  
For answering my prayer----AMEN:  
By: Reginald Taylor
